Leo (7.23 ~ 8.22)

Background

Legend has it that the origin of Leo is related to Hercules.

Hercules was the illegitimate son of Zeus and mortals. He was born with incomparable divine power, and the queen Hera was also jealous. When Hercules was a baby, he put two giant snakes in the cradle, hoping to kill Hercules. Unexpectedly, Hercules grinned and squeezed them to death. Hercules was served from a young age. As "the greatest hero of mankind."

Of course, Hera would not give up killing Hercules because of one failure. She deliberately made Hercules go crazy and beat her wife. After Hercules woke up, she was very regretful and sad, and decided to wash herself with penance. He came to Messinia and asked the king to send him a task. Who knew that the king was instructed by Hera to give him twelve missions that were as difficult as the sky. They must be completed within twelve days. One of them was To kill a cannibal lion.

This lion usually lives in the forest. Hercules went into the forest to find him, but the forest was silent. All animals, birds, deer, and squirrels were eaten clean by the lion. Hercules was tired of looking for him. Then I started to sleep. At this moment, the cannibal lion raised its head from a cave with a double opening. Kellers opened his eyes and saw that the cannibal lion was five times the size of a normal lion. It was covered with animal blood and added a few more. Subterror. Kles first shot him with a magical arrow, and then hit him with a wooden stick. It was useless. The cannibal lion was invulnerable. In the end, Hercules fought the lion hand-to-hand, the process was very tragic, but in the end he strangled the lion with brute force.

Although the cannibal lion died, Hera threw the cannibal lion into the air and became a Leo.
