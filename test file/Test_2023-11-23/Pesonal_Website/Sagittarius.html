Sagittarius
(11.22 ~ 12.21)

Background

Hercules, the great hero in ancient Greek mythology, accidentally injured his teacher Chiron in a process of hunting down the centaur robber, using the indestructible arrow given by the teacher. The arrow passed through the body of a centaur robber and hit Chiron's throat behind. Chiron was originally immortal, but Hercules' arrows were soaked in the blood of Hydra (also translated as Hydra), and Chiron felt more pain than death. At that time, Prometheus was tied to the Caucasus Mountains and tortured by Zeus because he had stolen the sky fire for human use. One day, Hercules came here in search of Hesperides. He saw the evil eagle pecking at Prometheus' liver. At this moment, he took out the bow and arrow and shot the evil eagle from the side of Prometheus' liver. Then he loosened the chains, freed Prometheus, and took him off the cliff. But in order to meet the conditions of Zeus, Hercules left Chiron on the cliff as a stand-in. Although Chiron could ask for eternal life, he was willing to give his life because he suffered from the venom of Hydra in order to save Prometheus. Chiron decided to exchange himself with Prometheus, freeing Prometheus from suffering and giving up eternal life, so that both parties could be free. Zeus ascended to the sky and became a Sagittarius holding a bow and arrow.

Character

Sagittarians are loyal, generous and unrestrained, energetic, controversial, quick-tempered, ambitious for power, and compassionate towards people who are suffering and oppression. In terms of character, he is honest, sincere, frank and trustworthy. In terms of temper, he is kind and generous to his friends, is very smooth in many things, and knows diplomacy very well. Strong mental strength, hate all indecent behavior, hate coarse things, aristocratic people, appearance and attitude are very bright and encouraging, with personality and courage, brave, strong self-esteem. Love freedom, very democratic in nature, and like to make friends.

Have a good sense of humor, determination and willpower, but become very stubborn when angry. Have good observation, comprehension and reasoning skills. The mind reacts quickly and learns everything quickly. Have a flexible imagination for practical things. Be interested in things around you and be able to concentrate on it. Have intuition and insight into people's motives. Healthy, but a bit nervous.

Feelings are sometimes enthusiastic and sometimes indifferent. This kind of fluctuating relationship is the cause of failure. Usually there will be more than one relationship and will be considerate to your partner.

Strong intuition and ability to foresee. There are noble ideals, high dreams, and luck is produced by self-trust. You have a tendency to spread your energies into multiple tasks, and you are unwilling to focus on a single goal. Having the special ability to deal with emergencies, a sudden accident often pushes you further to the realm of success. People who like to discuss and debate with others, like to study philosophy.

Advantages

Innately optimistic, full of ideals in life, honest and frank, rich sense of humor, love for peace, friendly to others, strong in action, own philosophy of life, can withstand blows, enthusiasm to save the world, optimistic, lively, frank, self-esteem Strong, versatile, highly intelligent, intuitionistic, capable of inspiring others, open-minded, adaptable, good judgment, capable of handling urgent matters, good at philosophical thinking, and a lofty sense of justice , Very sensitive and intelligent, loves freedom, sincerity, integrity, reliability, and prudence.

Humor. Move quickly and freely. Innovative type: Funny and humorous Sagittarius is the most humorous zodiac sign, and often brings happiness to everyone. Sagittarius belongs to the action school. The action is as fast as an arrow, and the things you think will be implemented immediately. At the same time I like to take risks and try a variety of new things, and I am very eager to explore.

Disadvantages

Careless, outspoken, easy to offend people, lack of patience, ignorance of the world, impulsive behavior, not knowing how to think twice, not believing in evil, not listening to advice, over-idealized, unrealistic, lack of step-by-step planning, too much emotion and anger Color, sensitive personality, a little impetuous, bragging, exaggerated, unresponsible, capricious, prone to restlessness, blindly and overly optimistic, careless, extreme, and a little clumsy.

Lack of patience. Easy to change your mind. Lack of responsibility: When a Sagittarius sees the target, he will quickly complete it, but if he fails several times in the process, he will give up and choose new things to play. Because he doesn't like restraint, he will always act Free-spirited type, often friends or people can not affect him, so there is no sign of family concept in this comparison.
