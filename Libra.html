Libra (9.23 ~ 10.20)

Background

Libra is a balance used by the goddess of justice Astoria in Greek mythology to judge good and evil for human beings. Astoria holds a balance in one hand and a sword to cut away evil in the other. In order to be fair, all eyes are blindfolded.

In the past, the gods and mankind co-existed peacefully on the earth. Although gods had eternal life, human life was limited. Therefore, the lonely gods can only continue to create mankind. However, at that time, people were fighting and evil karma was rampant. The gods returned to heaven after being disappointed with mankind.

Only the goddess Astoria is reluctant to go back and stay in the world, teaching others to be good. Despite this, mankind continued to fall, so the war started and began to fight and kill. In the end, even Astoria gave up human beings and returned to the sky. And the sky is high with Libra who loves justice, peace and justice.
