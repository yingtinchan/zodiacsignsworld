Aquarius (1.20 ~ 2.19)

Background

The water carrier represented by the zodiacal constellation Aquarius is Ganymede, a beautiful Phrygian youth. Ganymede was the son of Tros, king of Troy (according to Lucian, he was also the son of Dardanus). While tending to his father's flocks on Mount Ida, Ganymede was spotted by Zeus. The king of gods fell in love with him and flew down to the mountain in the form of a large bird, whisking Ganymede away to the heavens. Ever since, the boy has served as cupbearer to the gods. Ovid has Orpheus sing the tale.

Character

Aquarius people are quite intellectual, and have sharp observation, speculation ability, and a pioneering spirit full of adventure. After the thirst for knowledge is gradually strengthened, they will think deeply about everything. They will always have a skeptical attitude or unbalanced attitude towards all kinds of inequalities and contradictions in society, and gradually tend to anti-system thinking. As a result, they sometimes even become lonely heretics or show more aggressive actions. Individuality is independent and persistent. There are often some radical and innovative insights, people who belong to the new era, have rich compatriot love and democratic consciousness, can break social class and ethnic differences, and cultivate true friendship. For some established ideas, in order to be loyal to their beliefs, they will try to resist with excitement. This type of person is often seen in people who form parties and initiate campaigns in order to achieve a common purpose.

Aquarius people are fickle, sometimes lively, expressive, funny, charming, and sometimes depressed, indifferent and lazy. Charming demeanor, sometimes conservative, courageous, but quite capable of confession, honest and loyal friend, altruism is one of the highest main characteristics, has a humanitarian spirit, is arrogant, stubborn and independent , With an adventurous spirit.

Aquarius is emotionally sensitive and easy to get hurt, which can lead to blatant anger, but it will calm down quickly and will not hold a grudge for long. Have a lively mind and show extraordinary judgment, but under the influence of feelings and prejudice, judgment will become very poor. Has extraordinary sharp observation ability and can pay attention to details. The ability to analyze, synthesize, and classify is also very fast, and you can take action before others think about it. This kind of reaction is good, but it is bad only when you use your own feelings.

Aquarius has a natural rational understanding of the nature of the human heart, and at the same time has a high degree of tolerance, and the personality is usually balanced and healthy. Full of research spirit, love all new things, and be able to accept new discoveries. Having good observation skills and the ability to build theories often leads to a genius, inventor, scientist or a shrewd researcher of human behavior. However, in order to achieve the goal, we will not resort to violent and extreme methods, and will support the self-determination of citizens with a moderate attitude.

Aquarius is not easily affected by the surrounding environment, and only relies on his mental and spiritual abilities, does his best for himself, and acts in accordance with his own wisdom. The way to solve the problem is reasonable, but sometimes your opinion may not be realistic, because your vision is so far that it seems impossible to realize it in a short time. An Aries person has a positive and mobile personality. The personal, social, and economic aspects of his life are all determined by his personal mental and emotional performance. He is a passionate figure with a sense of justice, lively and bright, with charming manners and good mental ability, likes to be respected, good at diplomacy, has an adventurous and entrepreneurial spirit, loves various activities, is kind, has a chivalrous heart, and is generous temperament.
